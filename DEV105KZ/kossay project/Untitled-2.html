Biographie

MADD, born Mehdi rebati on Septamber 1, 1995, in casablanca, morocco, is leading figure in the moroccan rap scene. Growing up in the vibrant cultural envirenment of the old MEDINA, MADD was inspired by glonal rap legends like kendrick lamar while infusing his music with local culturzl elements.

Career

MADD began his journey in 2017 with the breakout single "3310", quicly gaining recognition. In 2020, he released his first studio album, Black rose, which featured collaboration with Lacrim, laylow and Xcep. His storytelling, blending Morrocan and international hip-hop, cemented his place as a top artist in the region.
